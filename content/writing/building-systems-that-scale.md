# Building Systems That Scale

Scale is not just about handling more users or processing more data. True scalability encompasses technical performance, organizational capacity, and the ability to evolve gracefully over time.

## The Three Dimensions of Scale

1. **Technical Scale**: Can the system handle increased load?
2. **Organizational Scale**: Can the team maintain and evolve it?
3. **Purpose Scale**: Does the system remain aligned with its original intent as it grows?

## The Human Element

No system exists in isolation. Every technical system is embedded in a social system, and understanding this relationship is crucial for building systems that truly scale.
